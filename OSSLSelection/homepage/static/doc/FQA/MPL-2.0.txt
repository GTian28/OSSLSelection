font-size:12pt; background-color:#ffffff"><span style="font-family:'微软雅黑 Light'; font-weight:bold; color:#222222">16. "minified" JavaScript</span><span style="font-family:'微软雅黑 Light'; font-weight:bold; color:#222222">是</span><span style="font-family:'微软雅黑 Light'; font-weight:bold; color:#222222">源代码</span><span style="font-family:'微软雅黑 Light'; text-align:justify; font-size:12pt; background-color:#ffffff"><span style="font-family:'微软雅黑 Light'; font-weight:bold; color:#222222">18.MPL应该用于非软件作品吗?</span></p><p style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt;
